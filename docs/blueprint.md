# **App Name**: Internet 8

## Core Features:

- Scenario Selection: Allows users to choose from various simulated real-world scenarios, such as online banking, suspicious SMS, or fake government websites, to practice internet safety.
- Interactive Internet Simulation: Provides an environment where users can navigate fake web pages and interactive interfaces designed to mimic common online experiences, including those with potential risks.
- Scam Anomaly Detection & Reporting: Enables users to identify and report 'anomalies' or simulated scam elements that appear within the interactive scenarios, reinforcing their awareness of deceptive practices.
- AI-Powered Guidance Tool: Utilizes Gemini as a tool to provide simple, context-aware explanations and safety tips in plain language, assisting users with inquiries like 'how to stay safe when using online banking.'
- Accessibility Text Sizing: Offers a prominent button to dynamically increase or decrease text size throughout the application, enhancing readability for users with varying visual needs.
- Progress & Attempt Tracking: Records user interactions, choices, and successful anomaly identifications within each simulation to provide a record of their learning progress, stored in Firebase.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A reassuring and professional medium blue (#2E81C0) to instill a sense of trust and calmness, representing digital guidance.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, blue-grey (#EEF4F9), maintaining a clean and expansive canvas that supports readability.
- Accent color: A vibrant aqua-cyan (#44CCCC) to highlight interactive elements, calls to action, and important information, ensuring clear visibility.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its high readability, modern appearance, and neutrality, ensuring clear communication across all content.
- Utilize clear, universally recognizable icons with simple lines. Avoid abstract or overly stylized graphics to ensure immediate comprehension and large tap targets for easy interaction.
- Emphasize a spacious and uncluttered layout with ample white space, large buttons, and clear hierarchy to minimize cognitive load and enhance ease of navigation.
- Implement subtle, functional animations such as smooth transitions for text size adjustments and gentle feedback on interactive elements, avoiding any flashy or distracting effects.

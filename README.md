## Problem Statement

Rapid digitalisation has shifted essential services such as banking, shopping, and government services to online platforms. 

Elderly individuals, especially people with low income, limited education, or physical limitations that often struggle to use the internet safely. 

The digital gap increases inequality and exposes them to scams, fraud, and misinformation.

---

## Sustainable Development Goal

This project aligns with **Sustainable Development Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities** by:

- Providing digital safe and accessible training
- Making online environments safer for elderly users
- Gain user confidence in using the Internet
- Reducing discrimination caused by poor accessibility design platforms

---

## Solution Overview

Internet 8 is a web-based training platform that allows users to practice real-world internet scenarios in a safe simulated environment.

Users can:
- Practice identifying suspicious messages 
- Learn safe online behaviours
- Assisted by AI-generated guidance
- Report "anomalies" during simulation
- Step-by-step instructions and explanations after each simulation
